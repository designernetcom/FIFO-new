ABOUT US

FD Group (FIFO Industries & 3D India Polymers) delivers high-precision injection moulds and plastic components with world-class engineering, advanced manufacturing, and ISO-certified quality.

Trusted by 300+ industries. FD Group was born in 2015 with a simple but powerful mission: to deliver precision-engineered moulds and plastic components that help manufacturers build world-class products.

What started as a dream of serving the plastic injection mold industry has now grown into a two-plant, ISO-certified organization trusted by 50+ clients across India and the globe.

At FIFO Industries, we focus on high-quality mold manufacturing, powered by advanced CNC/VMC machines, expert toolmakers, and strict quality control.

At 3D India Polymers, we transform those molds into superior plastic components using automated systems, modern injection molding machines, and an expert production team.

Together, we offer complete, end-to-end moulding solutions — from concept design and CAD modeling to tool building and mass production.
